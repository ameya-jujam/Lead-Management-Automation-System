# Lead Management Automation System

## Overview

A lead management and notification workflow built using n8n, Google Forms, Google Sheets, and Gmail API.

The system automatically collects lead information, stores it in a centralized database, segments leads based on predefined criteria, and triggers email notifications for follow-up actions. This helps businesses streamline lead handling and reduce manual administrative work.

---

## Problem

Businesses often collect leads through forms but manually process submissions, update spreadsheets, and notify team members. This creates delays and increases the chances of missing valuable opportunities.

---

## Solution

This workflow automates the lead management process by:

* Capturing lead information through forms
* Storing lead data in Google Sheets
* Categorizing leads based on age groups
* Sending automated notifications
* Alerting stakeholders when new leads are received

The result is a faster and more organized lead qualification process.
